Oh my deer

Personal illustration inspired by a photo found on Pinterest.
I played with negative space and chose pink tone of colors to emphasize the tenderness of the image.

Salto nel crudo

Identity for a collection of raw vegan video recipes contained in a Dvd.
I created a pattern illustration with vegetables and fruits, using bright colors to represent the freshness of the raw ingredients.
Design of the dvd case and booklet which contains recipes and a weekly menu.

Identità Preziose

Logo design for Silvana Zuanel, a wedding and event planner who asked for a logo that represents her activity called "Identità Preziose".
The brief was to use her letters name (S+Z) and play with ribbons or dragées elements combined in order to represent her love to details and elegance.
Branding and business card layouting in collaboration with Martina Doppio.

Diesel Tribute Catalogue

Design of Diesel Tribute lookbook for the collection designed by Nicola Formichetti, creative director of Diesel.
To give consistency trought the pages, I played with the key elements of the collection, such as lines of blue and pink colors as well as the textures which are printed on fabrics.
Project created during my internship at Diesel HQ in Breganze.
